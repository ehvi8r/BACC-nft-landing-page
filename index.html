About Bored Ape Cannabis Club

 

Bored Apes Cannabis Club is a collection of 9,985 script generated Apes and 15 Hand-created 'Genesis' Apes that are unique in a special way for a total of 10,000 Apes!

Each of the scripted Apes is created randomly from a selecion of various backgrounds, tops, glasses, smokeables and I threw in the BACC logo too. They were given rarity data, so some are more rare than others, and of course the Genesis Apes are one-of's, the rarity attribute will just show it is 1 of 15 hand made Apes. I made 1 super special just for Justin Beiber (sneak peak below), since I know he owns a Bored Ape, so you'll have to find it! Oh and he's Canadian, and this is kind of a Canadian thing since canabis became legal in Canada a couple of years ago, there is some specific Canadian content in some of the Apes too... some day the US and the rest of the world will follow!

These were developed by a team of one, yep, that's me, with a little help from The Peanut Gallery and Co. scripts from GitHub and a few great videos, a big shout out to the Peanut Gallery, you guys are awesome! And, another shout out to Jesse at CodeStackr also on GitHub with some great YouTube videos as well, thank you for the website template, and some great videos that helped make this project happen!
